Temperature vs Latitude

Temperature was plotted in Celsius on a range within -50 (-58 Fahrenheit) to 40 (104 Fahrenheit). Unlike the other resulting plots, in this case a clear correlation emerged between temperature and latitude. As expected and as shown, temperatures become warmer the closer a city is located to the equator.
